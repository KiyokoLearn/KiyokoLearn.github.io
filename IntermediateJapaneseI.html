Chapter 1

Note:
If you know all of the Hiragana, you can skip the first 5 lessons. However, keep in mind that the vocab and grammar from lesson 6 and on will be in Hiragana so you need to have a good grasp of Hiragana before you move on.

Lesson 1 -- The Writing System

- In this lesson you will be introduced to the three different writing systems used by the Japanese and what each is used for.

Lesson 2 -- The First of the Hiragana

- In this lesson you will be introduced to the first 20 of the Hiragana: a, i, u, e, o, ka, ki, ku, ke, ko, sa shi, su, se, so, ta, chi, tsu, te, to

Lesson 3 -- Even More Hiragana

- In this lesson you will learn the next 17 Hiragana: na, ni, nu, ne, no, ha, hi, fu, he ho, ma, mi, mu, me, mo, ya, yu, yo

Lesson 4 -- The Last of the Hiragana

- In this lesson you will learn the last of Hiragana: ra, ri, ru, re, ro, wa, wo, n

Lesson 5 -- Writing Rules and Exceptions

- In this lesson you will learn about tenten, maru, double consonants, and double vowels.

Lesson 6 -- Basic Grammar Structure

- In this lesson you will learn about basic sentence structure and particles.

Lesson 7 -- Well Hello There!! (Greeting and More)

- In this lesson you will learn how to greet others.

Lesson 8 -- Introductions


Lesson 9 -- Numbers

- In this lesson you will learn how to count to 1,000,000!

Lesson 10 -- Time

- In this lesson you will learn how to say and ask what time it is.

Lesson 11 -- School

- In this lesson we will learn school vocabulary.

Lesson 12 -- Family

- In this lesson we will learn family vocabulary.
